# paper delivery independence lock

Project: Browser-Enforced Security Policy

Working title: Policy Intent Drift in Browser-Enforced Security Policy Engineering

## Independence statement

This work is scoped as an original research artifact around policy intent drift for browser-enforced security policies. Its main problem formulation, corpus, abstraction, algorithm, evaluation questions, claim boundary, title angle, figure plan, artifact evidence, and written contribution are independent of any other project or manuscript.

## Locked independent research substance

* **Problem formulation:** explicit source-grounded policy intent can drift from browser-effective enforcement through delivery disposition, fallback/default behavior, request context, browser state, framework generation, rendering mode, and cross-policy composition.
* **Main corpus:** a locked source-grounded deterministic corpus of browser-policy evidence claims and fixtures.
* **Main abstraction:** BEP-IR linking source claims, generated header surfaces, context/state, browser-effective judgements, and semantic conflict witnesses.
* **Main algorithm:** deterministic witness generation and one-deletion witness minimization for the locked fragments.
* **Main evaluation:** locked semantic witness run, negative controls, baseline/control disagreement, ablations, robustness, scalability, and traceability validation.
* **Main claim:** minimal semantic conflict witnesses are a better evaluation object for browser-policy engineering than header-presence or single-policy checks alone.

## Non-overlap commitments

This manuscript must not share its core problem formulation, main corpus, abstraction, algorithm, evaluation question, claim, title angle, figure set, artifact evidence, or written contribution with any parallel manuscript. Broad motivation about software security, web security, or configuration errors may overlap with the general literature, but the research substance above is locked to this project only.

## Salami-slicing guard

The work must not be split into a separate benchmark-only paper, tool-report paper, framework-default survey, CSP-only study, CORS-only study, HSTS-only study, or checklist critique. The model, corpus, witness generator, evaluation, and discussion are maintained as one coherent research contribution.
